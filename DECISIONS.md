2026-05-24: Type the edges by interventionist signature, using Woodward's (M). Constitutive edges are identified by the FAILURE of surgical/independent intervention (you cannot move a directed obligation while holding its Hohfeldian correlative fixed; the change lands on both at once), so by Woodward's own criterion they are not causal. Reason: gives a principled test for edge type and explains why there is no fourth "constitutive intervention." Grounded in Woodward 2003 (direct-cause criterion) and 2014 §4 (only-through-X condition).

2026-05-24: Derive the three intervention types from which edge is perturbed: status-rule (normative-transition/constitutive edge; status moves, uptake follows), recognition (recognitional edge; uptake moves, status fixed), authorized-recognition (authority-conferring edge; both move). Set aside the pure causal intervention as not discriminating the coupled view from either reduction. Reason: makes the count of three principled and ties each type to a counterfactual signature and a carrying case; drafted into §5.

2026-05-24: Use Woodward's "methodology, not ontology" (2014) to rebut the "causal-normative network is just imprecise variable-talk" referee, and deploy his own discrimination disambiguation (6.2**: cause = the employer's beliefs about gender) as a recognition intervention that captures efficacy and drops validity. Reason: the strongest anticipated objection is interventionist; turning Woodward's non-reductive self-understanding against it shows the status variable is irreducible and previews the social-efficacy/moral-validity split for §6.

2026-05-24: Frame the network model-theoretically and add the model-theoretic-syntax precedent in §4 (one sentence + footnote). The network is a typed-edge relational structure specified by well-formedness constraints (a configuration is well-formed iff it models them), not a generative procedure; this follows Pullum & Rogers 2008 (CGEL formalized as a typed-edge structure, node-labels = categories, edge-labels = functions; their "function fusion" = our authorized-recognition node) and Pullum & Scholz 2001 (model-theoretic vs generative-enumerative). Reason: gives §4 a precise, tractable self-description tied to the CGEL category/function distinction. Caveat: borrow the conceptual framing only, not the MSO/decidability machinery, and keep grammatical well-formedness distinct from deontic validity.

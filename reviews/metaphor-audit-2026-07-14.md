## Abstract + §1 The problem of mixed explanation
**Dominant systems:** SOCIAL STATUS IS A POSITION, POSSESSION, OR BURDEN; CAUSAL AND INFERENTIAL DEPENDENCE IS MOTION THROUGH A NETWORK OR MECHANICAL TRANSMISSION; REPRESENTATIONAL ACCURACY IS TRACKING, FITTING, OR ANSWERING TO A TARGET; EXPLANATION IS A BUILT AND VISIBLE STRUCTURE
**Coherence:** The spatial-mechanical graph system largely coordinates the section: statuses occupy positions, recognitions connect to them, and causal or projective consequences move through displayed relations. Local strain appears when status-making alternates among transfer, coercive imposition, adhesion, and hardening, and when representation alternates among tracking, fitting, and answering.
**Risks:** Travel, driving, and firing can make defeasible projection look like automatic causal transmission rather than evidential licensing. Conferral, imposition, sticking, and hardening can also suggest that social efficacy suffices for status reality or validity, while fit and tracking may imply a uniquely pre-given normative target.

## §2 Why ordinary causal networks aren’t enough
**Dominant systems:** CAUSAL-NORMATIVE ORDER IS SPATIAL OR GRAPH STRUCTURE; INFERENCE IS FORWARD MOTION, PROJECTION, OR VISUAL READ-OFF; NORMATIVE STATUS IS A POSSESSED, BINDING, REMOVABLE OBJECT; CAUSAL OR NORMATIVE EFFICACY IS FORCE, COMBAT, PRODUCTION, OR MACHINERY
**Coherence:** The spatial-network system largely cooperates with the projection-and-path system: ordered dependencies provide routes for inference, while visual read-off marks correlativity as a different operation. Local collisions arise when duties alternate among the roles of restraint, possession, manufactured product, and combatant, and when normative fields are juxtaposed with causal machinery.
**Risks:** “Directed,” “from … to,” and “carried by links” can make asymmetry and transmissibility appear built into the phenomena rather than independently argued; “read-off” can likewise imply transparent, interpretation-free correlativity. “Field conditions” risks importing quasi-causal physics precisely where noncausal determination is claimed, while “defeats” and “gone” may overstate how completely release extinguishes a duty.
**Clashes flagged:** 4

- P25 (Release makes the point again. If...): The duty is both a restraint from which Nora is released and an opponent that release defeats; the physical-field and production metaphors add two further efficacy models.
- P26 (Consent has the same shape: withdraw...): Productive clash: causal order is a machine that keeps moving, whereas normative order is an arrangement imposed by withdrawal; the two senses of order compete over the same event.
- P27 (In what sense, then, are promises...): A duty is first a manufactured product and then a combatant that can be defeated, while its consequences alternately follow behind it and are drawn toward it.

## §3 Why normative taxonomies aren’t enough
**Dominant systems:** ABSTRACT NORMATIVE ORDER IS PHYSICAL STRUCTURE, MATERIAL, OR TERRAIN; NORMATIVE STATUS IS AN OBJECT OR SPATIAL POSITION; SOCIAL UPTAKE AND PROJECTION ARE OBJECT TRANSFER OR MOTION THROUGH A PATH; NORMATIVE EFFICACY IS FORCE AND NORM–CAUSE INTEGRATION IS MECHANICAL COUPLING
**Coherence:** Static cartography and structure depict what taxonomy captures, while transfer, travel, repair, and force supply the causal dynamics it omits. The closing coupling metaphor gathers these into a mostly coherent physical model despite local shifts of source domain.

## §4 The typed-edge model
**Dominant systems:** INFERENCE AND PROJECTION IS MOTION THROUGH A NETWORK; REPRESENTATION AND ASSESSMENT IS SEEING, DISPLAYING, TRACKING, AND READING; EXPLANATORY INTEGRATION IS ACCOUNTING; CAUSAL-NORMATIVE EFFICACY IS PRODUCTION, FORCE, MACHINERY, AND FLOW
**Coherence:** The visual-network system is largely coherent: profiles, tracking, paths, sources, targets, and read-offs jointly make typed inference inspectable. The accounting and machinery/cascade systems sharpen contrasts, but they sometimes collide with the travel model where noncausal status-determination is described through production, force, or propagation.
**Risks:** Travel, flow, cascade, and machinery can make projection seem like an entity transmitted through the world, importing causal direction or momentum into recognitional and status-determination relations even though projection is an analyst’s defeasible inference. ## §8 Conclusion
**Dominant systems:** NORMATIVE STATUS IS POSITION, POSSESSION, OR TRANSFERABLE OBJECT; INFERENCE AND SOCIAL EFFECTS ARE MOTION THROUGH ROUTES OR CASCADES; A MODEL IS A LOAD-BEARING STRUCTURE, MACHINE, OR WORKER; UNDERSTANDING IS SEEING, DISPLAYING, OR READING
**Coherence:** The systems mostly cooperate by making status assignments into sources whose consequences move through a visible, load-bearing network. Local collisions arise when the network alternates among conduit, building, map, machine, and worker, and when status or warrant alternates between place and object.
**Risks:** Carrier, cascade, and support language can make non-causal relations seem automatically transmissive or projectible merely because they occupy edges; the crucial licensing and stabilizing mechanisms still require independent argument. “Stabilized by field practices” risks moving from persistence to projectibility or from conventional acceptance to normative warrant, while “idle,” “collapse,” and “flattening” rhetorically prejudge decompositional rivals.

## Whole-paper synthesis

**The governing system holds.** Two mappings organize the entire paper and cooperate everywhere the auditors looked: INFERENCE IS TRAVEL THROUGH A NETWORK and UNDERSTANDING IS SEEING/DISPLAY. These are not decoration; they are the paper's model, thematized in the text itself ("the graph is a representation"; "the profile becomes explicit only when displayed"). The paper-wide census of load-bearing mappings (fit, vertical standing, transfer/imposition, reading an instrument, production, fastening) is what a typed-graph model of statuses should look like in prose.

**The one system-level risk tracks Reviewer B's old worry.** Five of eight auditors independently flagged the same slippage: travel, drive, fire, and cascade can make defeasible inference read as causal transmission. Checked against the text, the paper's discipline mostly contains this: "fire" and "drive" attach only to causal edges, "cascade" is defined as a response chain (causal), and §4 explicitly subordinates the travel idiom to the inference-must-respect-edge-type rule. The one figure that deliberately crosses the causal/non-causal line is "projection travels", and the paper earns it by definition. Verdict: expression-level discipline is doing its job; no systemic repair needed.

**Genuinely useful local findings.** (1) The remedies paragraph, the manuscript's newest, piled earthworks + engineering + organic decay + living-status sources into one sentence (P118, the audit's densest collision); repaired same-day ("deliberately bringing about the decay..."). (2) The "outruns" quartet compresses four incommensurable assessments onto one racetrack schema; kept, because the incommensurability is secured two paragraphs earlier ("none entails the others") and the compression is worth the tension. (3) The status-making verbs mix transfer (confer), force (impose), and adhesion (stick); kept, because the mix tracks the literature's own dual picture (Ásta's conferral IS imposition of constraints) rather than confusion. (4) "A social position with teeth" and the stakes paragraph's "idle/collapse" vocabulary are deliberate vividness at load-bearing moments; kept.

**What the audit says about the JSO verdict.** Reviewer B read the apparatus as "appearance of theoretical depth". The metaphor census shows why that reading was available: the paper's abstractions ride on physical source domains almost continuously. But it also shows why the reading is wrong of the current draft: the load-bearing mappings are few, repeated, mutually consistent, and disciplined by explicit statements of what the model is (a representation, not a producer). A framework whose metaphors were doing the arguing would show the opposite signature: many novel one-off mappings at exactly the argumentative joints. The joints here (the Woodward tests, the read-off restriction, the assessment separations) are where the prose is most literal.
